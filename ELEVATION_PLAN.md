# Project Elevation Plan: Targeted e-Learning Product Marketing

**Theme:** Market Segmentation

## 1. Vision & Goal
Develop a 'Market Prioritization Dashboard' that ranks geographic segments based on demand and willingness to pay.

This plan aligns with the portfolio-wide goal of elevating projects from basic tutorials to Senior/Principal-level data science products characterized by rigorous experiment design, research-grade reporting, and software engineering best practices.

## 2. Elevation Backlog

The following phases outline the concrete steps required to elevate this project. They should be executed sequentially.

[ ] **Phase 1: Decision Criteria** - Formalize the market entry decision criteria (demand size, willingness to pay, fit).
[ ] **Phase 2: Segmentation Model** - Build a reproducible pipeline that ranks segments and includes uncertainty/variance estimates.
[ ] **Phase 3: Pipeline Refactoring** - Clean up the survey data processing code.
[ ] **Phase 4: Marketing Report** - Draft a paper-style report with concrete business recommendations and caveats.
[ ] **Phase 5: Prioritization Dashboard** - Build an interactive dashboard for marketing teams to explore the segments.
